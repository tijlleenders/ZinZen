ZinZen lets you plan your drive - so you can enjoy your life. A purpose planner.
Create your best authentic self - with others. And keep your data private by default.
From a bird's eye perspective: ZinZen is a platform for holistic self-direction and self-organization by collaborating individuals, teams and institutions.

How does ZinZen work?
1. Create goals for yourself, just like with any other todo-app. Swipe/right-click to edit. Tap/click for sub-goals.
2. ZinZen then:
- Helps turn big life goals into simple tasks and habits.
- Plans your calendar automagically.
- Connects you with others on shared or matching goals.
- Encourages and alerts you when promises are at risk.
Ultimately, ZinZen helps you create the future you want, while allowing you to relax and fully enjoy every moment of the present.

Who owns ZinZen?
ZinZen will be steward-owned. This means:
1. All decisions are made by the stewards - and the stewards only.
2. All decisions must be in the best interests of the company's purpose.
Consequences:
- ZinZen's decision power can never be sold or inherited.
- All net profits go to making ZinZen better.
NB: Each investment round is capped.
Once a predetermined (decent) % is reached - the round ends.

Who develops ZinZen?
At the moment I'm a single non-pro developer challenging established companies that have tens to thousands of employees.
Please help ZinZen by giving suggestions, skills and/or money!
The goals I've defined so far can be found on my public ZinZen agenda.
